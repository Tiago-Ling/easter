How to play 🐣

  • Complete a task and tick it off to earn 10 points.
  • Each task has bonus challenges — tap "Bonus challenges" to expand them. Tick the ones you completed before (or after) marking the task done.
  • Only ticked bonuses add to your score.
  • iOS: Click the "..." -> "Share" -> "Add to Home Screen" if you want the app to run in fullscreen (no browser bars).
